An Introduction to me

“Success consists of moving from failure to failure without a loss of enthusiasm.”
-Often attributed to Winston Churchill
This is a quote that continues to help me to this day.

I Spent my early 20's working for a software company that developed a system that did inventory,
management, Accounting, and some customer relationship management or CRM tasks.
I took a break from formal education to pursue an opportunity.

The company gave me responsibilities that included sequenced query language (SQL) development, sales, and implementation of their software.

I traveled to countries around the world where I worked with companies to install and troubleshoot the software, while keeping up with my tasks in the different time zones.
I held this position for four years.

When the company opened a New York office, I was asked to help run it
and more than that I got to travel the world doing so.

I returned to New York self-assured, yet one piece was missing.
Although I had honed many skills and built rapport with customers, I wanted education.
So, I decided I was ready to return to an academic environment.

I left my position at Fantasy in [2018] and enrolled at the Borough of Manhattan Community College.
Over the last two years I have continued to build on my knowledge, gained new friends, and have matured in ways that leave me inspired for the future.

I seek to further my career in computer science and mathematics, and the opportunity to learn in a challenging environment, and to work with others at the top of my field.
